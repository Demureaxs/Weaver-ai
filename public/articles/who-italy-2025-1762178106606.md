### The WHO Italy 2025 Focus: Strengthening Health Systems

A primary goal for the **who italy 2025** partnership is to continue strengthening the country's national health service, the *Servizio Sanitario Nazionale* (SSN). After the immense pressures of the past few years, the focus is squarely on building resilience and modernizing services. This means investing in primary care to make it more accessible, enhancing digital health infrastructure, and ensuring the healthcare workforce is well-supported. According to the [WHO's European Programme of Work](https://www.who.int/europe/about-us/our-work/european-programme-of-work), empowering countries through better health information and digital solutions is a top priority. For Italy, this translates into creating more patient-friendly online portals and telehealth services. Making health information easy to navigate online is crucial, a principle that highlights the importance of excellent [Web Design](/services/web-design) in the public health sector. The goal is to create a system that is not only prepared for future crises but also better serves the daily health needs of everyone in Italy.
