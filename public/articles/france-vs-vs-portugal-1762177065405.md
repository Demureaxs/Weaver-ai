### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1: What is the head-to-head record between France and Portugal?**
Historically, France has had the upper hand in this fixture. Across all competitions and friendlies, France has won the majority of the matches. However, Portugal's victory in the Euro 2016 final stands as their most significant and memorable win against the French.

**Q2: What was the most important France vs Portugal match?**
Without a doubt, the most important match was the Euro 2016 final. Portugal's 1-0 victory in extra time gave them their first-ever major international trophy and did so on French soil, making it a monumental moment in the history of the rivalry.

**Q3: Who are some of the legendary players who have defined this rivalry?**
The rivalry has been marked by some of the game's greatest players. For France, legends like Michel Platini, Zinedine Zidane, and Thierry Henry have been key figures. For Portugal, icons like Eusébio, Luís Figo, and of course, Cristiano Ronaldo, have been central to the narrative.

**Q4: Have France and Portugal ever met in a World Cup Final?**
No, France and Portugal have never faced each other in a FIFA World Cup Final. They have met twice in the semi-finals (in 1984 and 2006), with France winning on both occasions.
